See all coverages

Here are some of the coverages we offer.

Liability

Provides coverage in case you are responsible for an accident.

Liability coverage handles the expenses associated with property damage and injuries you may be responsible for while driving, including court and attorney fees.

Comprehensive and collision coverage.

Safeguards your vehicle against unforeseen events.

Comprehensive coverage takes care of repairing or replacing your vehicle in case of damage caused by theft, vandalism, collisions with animals, fire, or natural disasters. Additionally, it covers any damage to your vehicle's glass.

Collision coverage pays for damage to your car, no matter who is at fault in the accident.

Uninsured/underinsured motorist

UMC and UIM can cover your medical bills, lost wages, and other expenses related to an accident, even if the other driver is uninsured or underinsured. They can also cover the cost of repairing or replacing your car.

Onside assistance

Pays for towing services and more

The specific services that are covered by a roadside assistance plan will vary from plan to plan. It is important to read the terms and conditions of the plan carefully to understand what is covered.

The cost of roadside assistance plans also varies. Some plans are offered by car manufacturers or insurance companies, while others are offered by third-party providers. The cost will depend on the level of coverage and the number of vehicles covered.

WHY DO YOU NEED CAR INSURANCE?

Insurance is required in nearly every state to protect you financially if you injure someone or damage their property. Your car insurance can also help pay to repair or replace your vehicle. There are ways to get cheaper car insurance without sacrificing your coverage.

How much does car insurance cost?

Car insurance rates are based on a variety of factors, such as age, location, driving record, vehicle type, and usage. The average cost of car insurance can vary depending on the region, but it is typically between $500 and $2,000 per year.

What do I need to get an auto insurance quote?

To get started, you need to provide:

  • Your car's make, model, and year
  • Some information about your driving history


To purchase an auto policy, you will need your vehicle identification number (VIN) and driver's license number. If you are unsure about which coverages to select, you can use our car insurance calculator to estimate your coverage needs.
